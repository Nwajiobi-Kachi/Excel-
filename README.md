# **🍕 PizzaHut Performance Analysis**
---

## **Objective**

To perform a comprehensive analysis of Pizza-Hut sales of 2015, uncover performance trends, identify top-performing pizzas and categories, and derive actionable insights using Excel.

---

## **Tools & Techniques**

* **Tool Used**: Excel  
* **Processes**:
  * Using Power Query to perform **ETL(Extract, Transform, and Load the data)**, creating new columns such as:  time of day, day/month name.  
  * Created clean and insightful **DAX measures** for quantity variance, revenue growth, and KPI tracking.  
  * Applied **conditional formatting, slicers, and filters** to allow smooth exploration.  
  * Designed visuals with **storytelling flow**, enabling users to navigate from macro to micro insights.

---

## **Key Insights**

### 🍕 **Sales Performance Overview**

* **🍕 21,350 orders → $817,860 revenue**  
* **𝐄𝐯𝐞𝐧𝐢𝐧𝐠𝐬 𝐝𝐨𝐦𝐢𝐧𝐚𝐭𝐞 — Marketing, staffing, and inventory should lean heavily into afternoons and evenings.**  
* **𝐅𝐫𝐢𝐝𝐚𝐲𝐬 𝐰𝐢𝐧 — Orders climb steadily through the week and peak on Friday (3,359 orders) before dipping over the weekend. People treat pizza as a weekend warm-up food.**
* **𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐒𝐭𝐚𝐫 𝐨𝐟 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐒𝐡𝐨𝐰: The Classic Deluxe stands out as the best seller (2,453 units sold _ Contributes 5% of total units)** 
* **The Classic pizza category is dominating 30% of units sold.**

## **Conclusions & Recommendations**

* Put in more man-power on Fridays, especially in the evening, because of the increased flow of orders.  
* To keep a higher percentage of our customer orders fulfilled, the classic pizza category should always be in stock **.
